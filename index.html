Welcome!

I'm Bryce, a senior at UC Berkeley double-majoring in Computer Science and Data Science with a domain emphasis in Economics. Currently, my main interests lie in computer and network security, specifically in transport layer protocols and decentralized systems. I also work on full-stack development, though my recent projects focus on frontend engineering. In my free time, I enjoy reading webcomics, watching basketball and competing on Berkeley's collegiate osu! team (VOD).
